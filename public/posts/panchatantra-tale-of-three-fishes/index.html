Tale of Three Fishes

#Panchatantra

Three little fish, Ana, Prat, and Yadbhav, lived in a pond with lots of other fish.

One day, some fishermen came by. They saw the pond was full of yummy fish! They decided to come back in the morning.

Ana and Prat heard the fishermen talking. They were scared! “The fishermen are coming back!” cried Ana. “We need to leave!” Prat agreed, “Let’s go to a new pond!”

Yadbhav laughed. “Silly fish!” he said. “This is our home! We’ve always lived here, and nothing bad has ever happened. Let’s stay!”

Some fish followed Ana and Prat to a new pond. Others stayed with Yadbhav.

The next morning, the fishermen came back. They threw their nets into the pond and caught all the fish that were still there! Poor Yadbhav and his friends!

Ana and Prat were safe in their new home.

The story reminds us: If you see danger, it’s good to get ready to be safe!
