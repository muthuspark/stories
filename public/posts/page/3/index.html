Mommy's Love

A little bird lived in a beautiful nest in a mango tree. She had tiny babies who couldn’t fly yet. The mommy bird worked hard to bring them food every day.

One day, it started to rain very hard. The baby birds got very hungry and started crying loudly. The mommy bird tried to comfort them, but they were too hungry to stop crying!

Munna's Three Rabbits

Munna had three cute, little rabbits. He loved them very much! Every day, before going to school, Munna would go to the park and bring back fresh, green grass for his bunnies to eat. Then he’d go to school and bring more grass for them when he came home.

Pride goeth before a Fall

Once upon a time, in a small village, lived ten cloth sellers who were best friends and always traveled together. One day, they went on a long trip to sell their beautiful fabrics and earned lots of money! As they headed back home, they had to pass through a thick, spooky forest.
